## Why GRC Matters for a SOC Analyst

A SOC analyst who understands GRC knows:
- **Why** a control exists — not just how to monitor it
- **What framework** maps to each alert they triage
- **What the business risk is** if a detection is missed
- **How to communicate findings** in audit and compliance language

This portfolio documents that context — built around a fictional fintech company called **CloudPay.**

## Fictional Company — CloudPay

All documents in this portfolio are built around CloudPay — a fictional Indian fintech payment platform. This scenario-based approach mirrors real GRC analyst work.

**CloudPay profile:**
- Industry: Fintech / Digital Payments
- Data processed: Payment data, PII, OTP/SMS
- Applicable frameworks: SOC2, NIST CSF, DPDPA 2023, PCI-DSS
- Team size: 50–200 employees
- Cloud provider: AWS
